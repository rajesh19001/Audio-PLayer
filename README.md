# PureAudio Player 🎵

**"PureAudio: For those who don't just hear music, but feel every beat."**

PureAudio is a minimalist, high-performance open-source music player designed for audiophiles. It features a custom multi-pass lyrics engine, a dynamic theme system, and professional queue management.

## 🚀 Key Features
* **Triple-Pass Lyrics Engine:** An advanced scrubber that automatically removes messy metadata tags (like *Naasongs*, *TeluguWap*, or *320kbps*) to find the correct synced lyrics.
* **Interactive Synced Lyrics:** View real-time karaoke-style lyrics and tap any line to instantly jump the music to that exact second.
* **Dynamic Appearance:** Switch between 5 premium themes (Cyber Lime, Electric Blue, Crimson Red, Amber, and Deep Purple) directly from the 3-dots menu.
* **Play Next Interaction:** Long-press any song in your library to instantly add it to the "Play Next" position in your current queue.
* **Offline Lyrics Cache:** Once lyrics are successfully found, they are saved to your device automatically so you can view them later without an internet connection.
* **Advanced Search:** Easily find full albums or movie soundtracks by searching for the movie name, artist, or track title.
